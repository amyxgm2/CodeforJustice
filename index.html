Who We Are

At Table for All, we believe that access to nutritious food is a basic human right, not a privilege. Based in Charlotte, North Carolina, our community-powered initiative connects residents with local food banks, affordable grocery options, and essential support services.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between food insecurity and accessibility by c connecting communities to local food banks, sharing affordable grocery solutions, and empowering families through knowledge, resources, and support.
