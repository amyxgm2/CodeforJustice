Project Overview:
The purpose of this project was to address food insecurity within the Charlotte metro area. Our goal was to provide resources that help individuals facing food insecurity locate food banks, find budget-friendly recipes, compare food prices, and learn more about our team.

Features:
The website includes a JavaScript form validation feature that ensures users enter the correct type of input for our "Get Involved" section.

Usage Guide:
The homepage welcomes visitors with a brief introduction to who we are and what we believe in. Our mission, values, and vision are presented clearly, followed by key statistics that highlight the widespread impact of food insecurity.

The next page is the Food Bank Locator, which displays accessible food banks in the Charlotte metro area. Each listing includes a photo, name, and address to make it easier for users to identify and navigate to the location.

Following that is the Recipe Page, featuring cost-effective meal ideas. These recipes are designed to use ingredients commonly found in food pantries or those that are affordable. The page is easy to use—users can swipe through recipe videos to explore their options.

Next is the Price Comparison Page, which allows users to compare prices for food essentials between Walmart and Target, helping them make more informed shopping decisions.

Lastly, we have the Our Team page, which features four cards—one for each group member. Each card includes the team member’s name and a short biography.
